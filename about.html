Authorities


Brig Abhay A Bhat

Director

Brig Abhay A Bhat, an alumnus of National Defence Academy (NDA) was commissioned into the Corps of Signals in December 1982. He has done his BTech in Electronics and telecommunications from Military College of Telecommunications Engineering, Mhow (MCTE). Brig Bhat has graduated from the prestigious Defence Services Staff College, Wellington, where army officers are trained in the nuances of staff function of various operations. Due to his professional calibre, he was sponsored by the Army to attain M Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT, Kanpur. The officer has also acquired a Master’s degree in Personnel Management from Pune University.

Col Manoj Kumar Prasad (Retd)

Joint Director

Col Manoj Kumar Prasad (Retd), an Engineering Officer served in Indian Army from Jun 1986 to May 2018. He superannuated from CME Pune after 32 years of experience in various combat and works appointments. He is an alumni of BIT Sindri, Gold Medalist of IISc Bangalore, Post Graduate Diploma in PM&HR from SIMS Pune, Diploma in Business Computing from CDAC besides alumnus of IIM Ahmedabad. He served 202 Engineer Regiment five tenures, in MES as SO2 (Design), CWE (Jabalpur), CWE (Vishakhapatnam), Additional Chief Engineer (Works) New Delhi, two tenures as Instructor at CME Pune and one tenure as instructor at MEG&C Bangalore. He served as University Engineer, Nalanda University and Col GS (Engrs), Strategic Force Command. He has privilege to serve Army/ Navy/ Air Force/ Strategic Force Command/ Nalanda University. After retirement from defence forces, he served as Project Head (Civil) at Sterlite Technologies Ltd (Vedanta Group) at Gurgaon and constructed 34 data centers PAN India for Indian Navy under project Varun..

Dr B P Patil

Principal

Received his Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics Engineering from Amravati University, India in 1990 and Masters in Engineering degree from MNREC (NIT) Allahabad, India in 1996. He received Ph.D. in Electronics Technology from Guru Nanak Dev Univ., Amritsar, India in year 2000. Along with this he also did DBM and MMS from North Maharashtra University in 1993 and 1994 respectively. He is having enriched teaching experience of 27 years and 5 years in industry. He has published 174 research papers in various international and national referred journals and conferences. He has filed 7 patents, five copy rights and published two books. He is recognized Ph.D. guide from University of Pune. Under his able guidance 10 students completed Ph.D. in engineering field. His area of Interest includes Wireless Communication, Wireless Sensor Network, Nonconventional energy and Signal processing.
